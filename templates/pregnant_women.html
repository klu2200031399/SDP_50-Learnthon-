Pregnant Women

Food Recommendations

Include a variety of nutrient-rich foods in your diet during pregnancy. Consult with your healthcare provider for personalized advice.

Exercise Tips

Engage in safe and moderate exercise to promote overall well-being during pregnancy. Consult your doctor before starting any new exercise routine.

Health Tips
